Conditions for Refund

At Picsi Drop, we strive for chromatic excellence. Refunds are considered under the following conditions:

  • 01. Project cancellation requested within 48 hours of the initial deposit and before any creative direction has commenced.
  • 02. Technical failure on our platform that prevents the delivery of final curated assets after all troubleshooting attempts.
  • 03. Failure to initiate a project within the agreed-upon timeline specified in your initial brief.

The Request Flow

01

Submission

Send an email to support@picsidrop.com with your project ID and the specific reason for your request. We value honest feedback.

02

Review

Our Chromatic Curator team will review your request against the project milestones reached. This takes roughly 3 business days.

Processing Times

Once approved, your refund is initiated immediately. However, the time it takes for funds to reflect in your account depends on your financial institution.
