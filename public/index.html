Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my parcel? expand_more
We prioritize security through a dual OTP verification system. Both the sender and recipient must verify unique codes at pickup and delivery. Additionally, all travelers undergo strict identity verification and real-time GPS tracking is available throughout the journey.
How do I become a traveler? expand_more
Becoming a traveler is easy! Download the app, complete your profile, and submit your government-issued ID for verification. Once approved, you can post your upcoming travel routes and start accepting delivery requests that match your journey.
What are the delivery fees? expand_more
Delivery fees are typically up to 70% lower than traditional courier services. The cost is calculated based on parcel size, weight, and distance. Since you're connecting with travelers already going that way, you bypass expensive logistics overheads.
What items are prohibited for delivery? expand_more
For safety reasons, we prohibit illegal substances, hazardous materials, flammable items, and restricted biological products. All travelers have the right to inspect parcels at pickup to ensure compliance with our safety guidelines.
Is there insurance for lost or damaged parcels? expand_more
Yes, Picsi Drop offers integrated protection for all verified deliveries. In the rare event of loss or damage, our community protection fund and insurance partnerships provide coverage up to a specified value, depending on your delivery plan.
